Our Menu

THE FANTASTIC FOUR

1. L'Offrande de la Mer

"A Symphony of Zucchini Ribbons and Seaside Graces" Meet L'Offrande de la Mer (The Ocean's Offering). This delicate ensemble combines the boldness of shrimp with the subtlety of zucchini ribbons, crowned by artfully toasted croutons and lush greens. A tribute to the sea and earth, it’s as sophisticated as it is refreshing.

2. Matin Exquis

"An Artisanal Morning Ritual with a Twist" Introducing Matin Exquis (Exquisite Morning), where poached eggs nest on crisp, golden-battered bread, accompanied by avocado slices adorned with chia jewels. Braised Roman beans lend a warm touch, crafting a regal start to your day that's both healthful and hearty.

3. Rustique de Potiron

"A Hearty Embrace of Sweet Earthiness and Rustic Aroma" Rustique de Potiron (Rustic Sweet Pumpkin Stew) brings together sweet potatoes and tender beef, slow-cooked to perfection in a rich, rosemary-infused stew. It’s comfort food with a countryside charm, sure to warm your spirit and soul alike.

4. Basically Pasta

"It’s Pasta. In Sauce. With Olives. Here it is, Basically Pasta. No frills, no fancy. Just noodles, tomato sauce, and a handful of olives tossed in for good measure. It’s straightforward, reliable, and as comforting as that one TV show you keep rewatching. Embrace the simplicity—sometimes, that’s all you really need."
